The C-Class. Never Stop Improving.

New E, New Attitude. The all-new Mercedes-Benz E-Class comes with stunningly elegant exteriors. With contemporary new headlights, power domes, and stylish bumper design. With MBUX NTG 6, the E-Class remembers everything; from your favourite routes to responding to your commands. Stay connected to your car from anywhere with Mercedes me connect, which is loaded with features like remote engine start, geofencing, and much more. Sit back and relax with the detachable rear tablet and the rear AC console with stunning new trims. The AMG line brings you plush beige and blue upholstery as well.

The new E-Class is practically unbeatable in matters of comfort. Air cooling, first-class sound provided by the Burmester® surround sound system or the ambient lighting sometimes make you forget that you are sitting in a car.
