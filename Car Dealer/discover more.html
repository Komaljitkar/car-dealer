MERCEDES

BEST CAR DEALER IN TOWN!

The modern automobile was invented by Karl Benz, with his patent of the “Motorwagen” in 1886. His wife funded his work. Her name was Bertha Ringer Benz, and she took the first road trip in a modern car – 130 miles round trip with her sons. The first car in the country of Nepal was a Mercedes-Benz, given to its king in 1940. The logo of the company represents air, land, and water. The company thought about adding a fourth point to the star to represent space, but decided not to.
